VELA · PREVIEWS · POST-V0 SURFACE

Workbench previews

Four proposed product surfaces. Not shipping v0.

These HTML files are design-bundle proposals from the Vela design system for what a future workbench GUI could look like. They are not wired to data, do not reflect shipping v0 functionality, and are published here only as a brand reference.

Vela v0 is a CLI protocol. See the landing page or run vela --help in a terminal.

01 · Frontier
The table of findings. Search, facet pills, sortable rows with engraved state chips.
02 · Finding
One finding read end to end. Serif claim, conditions, evidence, history ledger in the margin.
03 · Terminal
The CLI. Paper-toned, line-numbered, serif block-quotes for cited evidence, signal caret.
04 · Proof
The sealed document. Ceremonial plate, rete seal with alidade, human + agent signatures, audit panel.
