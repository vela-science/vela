# PICALM and LRP1 at the blood brain barrier

PICALM regulates clathrin-dependent internalization of amyloid-beta bound to LRP1 in brain endothelial cells. Loss of PICALM reduces amyloid-beta clearance across blood brain barrier models and may increase vascular amyloid burden. These claims are source notes for a local fixture and require review against the cited paper before scientific use.
